Ingredients :

- 1.5 pounds boneless skinless chicken breasts or thighs.
- 2 cups heavy cream.
- 1 cup low sodium chicken broth.
- 1 tablespoon butter chopped.
- 5 oz grated parmesan cheese.
- 3 tablespoons Cajun Seasoning.
- 2 cloves garlic minced.
- 1 teaspoon garlic powder.
- 1 teaspoon red chili flakes.
- 1/2 teaspoon salt.
- 1/2 teaspoon pepper.
- 1 head broccoli rinsed and trimmed.

Cornstarch Slurry :

- 1 tablespoon cornstarch.
- 1 tablespoon cold water.


Instructions :

1.Place heavy cream, chicken broth, parmesan, garlic powder, red pepper flakes, pepper, cajun seasoning, and salt in crockpot.
2. Whisk ingredients together.
3. Add chicken breasts and cover with sauce.
4. Add butter on top of each breasts and minced garlic.
5. Depending on how you like your broccoli you can add at the beginning but it will be soft. If you don’t like broccoli soft add 30 minutes-1 hour before finished cooking or steam on its own.
6. Cook on low for 4-5 hours or high for 2-3 hours.
7. Make cornstarch slurry to thicken sauce. Mix 1 Tablespoon Cornstarch with 1 Tablespoon water and mix together in bowl until combined.
8. Remove chicken for crockpot and pour sauce into pan on stove and heat until sauce is simmering. Add cornstarch slurry. Cook until sauce thickens.
9. Pour sauce over chicken and pasta or rice.
